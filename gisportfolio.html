GIS Portfolio

This collection highlights a range of GIS projects that reflect my development as a cartographer. It includes both personal explorations and coursework from the Center for Geospatial Analysis at William & Mary, each contributing to my growing skills in spatial analysis, map design, and storytelling through data.

Automated Map Series

This project automates the creation of a multi-page map series using ArcPy in ArcGIS Pro. The script loops through features in a layer and generates consistent layouts for each, streamlining workflows for recurring mapping tasks. It was used here to produce a themed series of light pollution maps across five counties.
